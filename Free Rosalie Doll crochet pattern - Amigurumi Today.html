Free Rosalie Doll crochet pattern

June 2, 2021 by Amigurumi Today


Create a sweet amigurumi doll with our free Rosalie Doll Crochet Pattern. Choose your own color palette for the doll’s skin, hair and outfit!

Sign up to find step-by-step instructions below!

To crochet Rosalie doll you need:

    • Semi-cotton yarn Yarnart Jeans (160 m / 50 g; 55% cotton, 45% acrylic) or Alize Cotton Gold
    • 1.5 mm hook for the doll itself and 2.5 mm hook for the outfit
    • Fiberfill for stuffing
    • 10 mm safety eyes
    • Some embroidery thread for eyes, mouth and eyebrows
    • Some plastic or cardboard for shoe insoles
    • Five 5-6 mm buttons or beads
    • Coated copper wire (diameter: 2-2.5 mm)
    • Adhesive tape
    • Scissors, pliers and wire cutter
    • Sewing and tapestry needle

Skill level: advanced

The finished amigurumi doll is about 26 cm (10″) tall, if you use the same supplies. The doll crochet pattern also works well with thinner or thicker yarn, but your doll size can be different.

Rosalie Doll Crochet Pattern
Free Amigurumi Pattern

Abbreviations:
ch = chain
st = stitch
sc = single crochet
inc = increase (2 stitches in one)
dec = decrease (2 stitches together)
sl st = slip stitch
dc = double crochet
hdc = half double crochet
(..) = repeat the instructions in brackets the given number of times
[..] = total number of stitches

Crochet the doll in continuous rounds with 1.5 mm hook. Mark the beginning of each round with stitch marker.

Arms (make 2):
Crochet with yarn in skin color.
Rnd 1: 6 sc in magic ring [6]
Rnd 2: (inc) repeat 6 times [12]
Rnd 3: (sc in next 3 st, inc) repeat 3 times [15]
Rnd 4-6: sc in all 15 st [15]
Rnd 7: bobble stitch consisting of 4 hdc, sc in next 14 st [15]
Rnd 8: (dec, sc in next 3 st) repeat 3 times [12]
Rnd 9: (sc in next 2 st, dec) repeat 3 times [9]
Rnd 10: sc in all 9 st [9]
Rnd 11: (sc in next 2 st, inc) repeat 3 times [12]
Stuff with fiberfill.
Rnd 12-31: sc in all 12 st [12]
Stuff a little bit more so that the upper part of the arm is empty. Finish with sl st and fasten off.

Legs (make 2):
Crochet 9 ch with white yarn. Continue working around the foundation chain, beginning from 2nd ch from hook.
Rnd 1: inc, sc in next 6 st, 4 sc in next st, sc in next 6 st, inc [20]
Rnd 2: (inc) x 2 times, sc in next 6 st, (inc) x 4 times, sc in next 6 st, (inc) x 2 times [28]
Rnd 3: (sc in next st, inc) x 2 times, sc in next 6 st, (inc, sc in next st) x 4 times, sc in next 6 st, (sc in next st, inc) x 2 times [36]
Now you have a crochet sole of your boot. Cut out two insoles from cardboard or plastic piece using this sample. The cardboard/plastic insole should be about 1-2 mm smaller than crochet sole on all edges.
Rnd 4: sc in all 36 st in back loops only [36]
Rnd 5-7: sc in all 36 st [36]
Put the cardboard/plastic insole inside the shoe.

Make sure that the yarn marker is in the middle of the heel (add or loose some stitches if needed).
Rnd 8: sc in next 11 st, (dec) x 7 times, sc in next 11 st [29]
Rnd 9: sc in next 9 st, (dec) x 5 times, sc in next 10 st [24]
Rnd 10: sc in next 9 st, (dec) x 3 times, sc in next 9 st [21]

Preparing a wire frame:
* Bend a 26 cm piece of wire about 1-1.5 cm from the edge.
* Insulate the frame with adhesive tape so that the wire cuts are covered.
* Attach the insulated end of the wire to the insole with the adhesive tape.

Stuff with fiberfill after inserting the frame. Add more stuffing as you go.

Rnd 11: sc in next 10 st, dec, sc in next 9 st [20]
Rnd 12-13: sc in all 20 st [20]
Rnd 14: sc in all 20 st in front loops only [20]
Fasten off and weave in the end.

Attach a yarn in skin color to a back loop of Rnd 13.
Rnd 15: sc in all 20 st in back loops of Rnd 13 [20]
Rnd 16-31: sc in all 20 st [20]

Make sure that yarn marker is located in the middle of the back part of the leg (add or loose some stitches if needed). Change to white yarn.
Rnd 32-34: sc in all 20 st [20]
Make second leg in the same way, but don’t cut off the yarn, you will continue working on the body with it.

Body:
Begin working on the body from middle point of the inner thigh of the second leg (add or loose some stitches if needed).
Make 1 ch from second leg and join it with the first leg as follows:
Rnd 1: sc in next 20 st on first leg, sc in next ch, sc in next 20 st on the second leg, sc in next ch (opposite side of the chain) [42]

Rnd 2: sc in next 3 st, inc, (sc in next 6 st, inc) x 5 times, sc in next 3 st [48]
Join leg wire frames with adhesive tape.
Rnd 3-4: sc in all 48 st [48]
Rnd 5: (sc in next 7 st, inc) repeat 6 times [54]
Rnd 6-13: sc in all 54 st [54]
Rnd 14: (dec, sc in next st) x 2 times, dec, sc in next 37 st, dec, (sc in next st, dec) x 2 times, sc in next st [48]
Rnd 15: sc in next 7 st, dec, (sc in next 14 st, dec) x 2 times, sc in next 7 st [45]
Rnd 16: (dec, sc in next 13 st) x 3 times [42]

Make sure that the yarn marker is in the middle of the back (add or loose some stitches if needed). Change to yarn in skin color.
Rnd 17: sc in next 6 st, dec, (sc in next 12 st, dec) x 2 times, sc in next 6 st [39]
Rnd 18-29: sc in all 39 st [39]

Make sure that the yarn marker is in the middle of the back (add or loose some stitches if needed).

Attach arms while working on the next round. Flatten the edges of the arms so that the thumbs point forward.
Rnd 30: sc in next 6 st, sc in next 6 st of both the arm and the body, sc in next 15 st of the body only, sc in next 6 st of both the other arm and the body, sc in next 6 st of the body only [39]

Crochet next round on both the body and the arms, excluding those 12 st (6 st on each side), where you joined the parts together.

Rnd 31: sc in next 6 st of the body, sc in next 6 st of the arm, sc in next 15 st of the body, sc in next 6 st of other arm, sc in next 6 st of the body [39]
Rnd 32: (sc in next 11 st, dec) repeat 3 times [36]

Trim the frame with wire cutter leaving 8 cm over the crocheted body. Seal the cut area with adhesive tape.

Rnd 33: sc in next 2 st, dec, (sc in next 4 st, dec) x 5 times, sc in next 2 st [30]
Rnd 34: (sc in next 3 st, dec) repeat 6 times [24]
Rnd 35: (sc in next 2 st, dec) repeat 6 times [18]
Rnd 36-37: sc in all 18 st [18]
Finish with sl st and fasten off.

Head:
Crochet with yarn in skin color.
Rnd 1: 6 sc in magic ring [6]
Rnd 2: (inc) repeat 6 times [12]
Rnd 3: (sc in next st, inc) repeat 6 times [18]
Rnd 4: (sc in next 2 st, inc) repeat 6 times [24]
Rnd 5: (sc in next 3 st, inc) repeat 6 times [30]
Rnd 6: sc in next 2 st, inc, (sc in next 4 st, inc) x 5 times, sc in next 2 st [36]
Rnd 7: (sc in next 5 st, inc) repeat 6 times [42]
Rnd 8: sc in next 3 st, inc, (sc in next 6 st, inc) x 5 times, sc in next 3 st [48]
Rnd 9: (sc in next 7 st, inc) repeat 6 times [54]
Rnd 10: sc in all 54 st [54]
Rnd 11: sc in next 4 st, inc, (sc in next 8 st, inc) x 5 times, sc in next 4 st [60]
Rnd 12-25: sc in all 60 st [60]
Insert safety eyes between Rnd 21 and Rnd 22 (12 stitches in between the eyes).
Rnd 26: sc in next 9 st, dec, (sc in next 18 st, dec) x 2 times, sc in next 9 st [57]
Rnd 27: (sc in next 17 st, dec) repeat 3 times [54]
Rnd 28: (sc in next 7 st, dec) repeat 6 times [48]
Rnd 29: sc in next 3 st, dec, (sc in next 6 st, dec) x 5 times, sc in next 3 st [42]
Rnd 30: ( sc in next 5 st, dec) repeat 6 times [36]
Rnd 31: sc in next 2 st, dec, (sc in next 4 st, dec) x 5 times, sc in next 2 st [30]
Stuff with fiberfill.
Rnd 32: (sc in next 3 st, dec) repeat 6 times [24]
Rnd 33: (sc in next 2 st, dec) repeat 6 times [18]
Add a little bit more stuffing. Finish with sl st and fasten off.

Hair:
1. Mark with pins the hair line.
2. Take the yarn you’ve chosen for hair. Cut the yarn into pieces (each piece is equal to double length of hair). Fold the piece in half and draw its center through the stitch of the head with your hook. Pick up 2 strands, draw them through the loop and tighten. Attach all other pieces in the same way, then trim the hair.

Ears (make 2):
Crochet 6 ch with yarn in skin color.
Row 1: sl st in 2nd ch from hook, sc in next ch, 2 hdc in next ch, sc in next ch, sl st in next ch [6]
Fasten off leaving a tail for sewing.

Doll assembly: 
1. Embroider eye whites, lashes and brows.
2. Embroider a nose using the yarn in skin color on Rnds 22-23.
3. Embroider a mouth 2 rounds below from the nose.
4. Sew ears to the head (6-7 stitches aside from the eyes).
5. Sew the head to the body.
